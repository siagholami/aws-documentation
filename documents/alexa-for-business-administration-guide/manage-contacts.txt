Managing Contacts
After you create contacts, you can add them to address books. Edit or delete a contact at any time.
To create a contact


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose Calls, Contacts.


Choose Create contacts, and enter a name, phone number, and optional description. 


To add more contacts, choose Add another contact.


Choose Add contacts.


To add contacts to address books


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose Calls, Contacts.


Select the names of the contacts to add to your address book and choose Add to address books.


Select the check boxes next to the address book to which to add the contacts and choose Add.


To edit a contact


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose Calls, Contacts, and select the contacts to edit.


Edit the values for Name, Phone number, and Description, and then choose Save.


To delete a contact


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose Calls, Contacts, and select the check box next to the contacts to delete.


Choose Delete contacts, Delete.
