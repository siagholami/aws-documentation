# Managing Contacts<a name="manage-contacts"></a>

After you create contacts, you can add them to address books\. Edit or delete a contact at any time\.

**To create a contact**

1. Open the Alexa for Business console at [https://console\.aws\.amazon\.com/a4b/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/)\.

1. Choose **Calls**, **Contacts**\.

1. Choose **Create contacts**, and enter a name, phone number, and optional description\. 

1. To add more contacts, choose **Add another contact**\.

1. Choose **Add contacts**\.

**To add contacts to address books**

1. Open the Alexa for Business console at [https://console\.aws\.amazon\.com/a4b/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/)\.

1. Choose **Calls**, **Contacts**\.

1. Select the names of the contacts to add to your address book and choose **Add to address books**\.

1. Select the check boxes next to the address book to which to add the contacts and choose **Add**\.

**To edit a contact**

1. Open the Alexa for Business console at [https://console\.aws\.amazon\.com/a4b/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/)\.

1. Choose **Calls**, **Contacts**, and select the contacts to edit\.

1. Edit the values for **Name**, **Phone number**, and **Description**, and then choose **Save**\.

**To delete a contact**

1. Open the Alexa for Business console at [https://console\.aws\.amazon\.com/a4b/](https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/)\.

1. Choose **Calls**, **Contacts**, and select the check box next to the contacts to delete\.

1. Choose **Delete contacts**, **Delete**\.
