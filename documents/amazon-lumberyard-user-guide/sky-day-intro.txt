Creating a Dynamic Daytime Sky
To add a dynamic daytime sky, you adjust various sun parameters, atmospheric properties, sun ray effect, and sun shadows. Dynamic skies use the SkyHDR Shader.
All properties and parameters in the following topics are ignored when you use a static sky (SkyBox), which uses the Sky Shader.
