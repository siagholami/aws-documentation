Enable Water Droplets
Apply a water effect that appears from various sources on the screen. Unlike the Enable Rain Drops node, this node simulates a splash of water thrown on the screen. For example, you can use this node when the camera leaves the water.
To disable the effect, see Disable Water Droplets.
