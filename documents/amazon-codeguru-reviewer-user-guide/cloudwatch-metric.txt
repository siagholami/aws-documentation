Monitoring profiling groups with CloudWatch metrics
You can view Amazon CodeGuru Reviewer metrics in the Amazon CloudWatch console. 
To access profiling group metrics


Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the CloudWatch console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudwatch/.


In the navigation pane, choose Metrics. 


On the All metrics tab, choose AWS/CodeGuruReviewer. 


Choose the dimension you want metrics for: ProviderType, CodeReviewType, or RepositoryName. The graph on the page displays metrics for recommendations for all selected items that are available for the selected dimension.
