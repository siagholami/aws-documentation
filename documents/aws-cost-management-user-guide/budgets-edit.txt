Editing a budget
You can't edit the budget name.
To edit a budget


Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the Billing and Cost Management console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/billing/home#/.


On the navigation pane, choose Budgets.


On the Budgets page, choose the budget that you want to edit from your list of budgets.


Choose Edit budget.


Change the parameters that you want to edit. You can't change the budget name.


Choose Configure alerts.


Choose Confirm budget.


Choose Done.
