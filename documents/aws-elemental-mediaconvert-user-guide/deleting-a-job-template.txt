Deleting a custom job template in AWS Elemental MediaConvert
You can delete any custom job template. You can't delete system job templates.
To delete a job template


Open the MediaConvert console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/mediaconvert.


On the navigation bar of the MediaConvert console, choose the region where the queue you want to delete was created.



Choose the three-bar icon on the left to access the left navigation pane.


Choose Job templates.


Choose the name of the custom job template that you want to modify.


By default, the Job templates page is filtered to show only custom templates.

On the Job template details page, choose the Delete button.
