Monitoring profiling groups with CloudWatch metrics
You can view Amazon CodeGuru Profiler metrics in the Amazon CloudWatch console. 
To access profiling group metrics


Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the CloudWatch console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudwatch/.


In the navigation pane, choose Metrics. 


On the All metrics tab, choose AWS/CodeGuruProfiler. 


Choose ProfilingGroupName. Metrics for recommendations for all selected profiling groups are displayed in the graph on the page.
