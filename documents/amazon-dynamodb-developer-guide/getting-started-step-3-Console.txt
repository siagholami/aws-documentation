AWS Management Console
Follow these steps to read data from the Music table using the DynamoDB console.


Open the DynamoDB console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/dynamodb/.


In the navigation pane on the left side of the console, choose Tables. 


Choose the Music table from the table list.


Choose the Items tab for the Music table.


On the Items tab, view the list of items stored in the table, sorted by Artist and SongTitle. 


The first item in the list is the one with the Artist Acme Band and the SongTitle Happy Day.
