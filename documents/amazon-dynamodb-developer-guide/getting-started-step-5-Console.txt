AWS Management Console
Follow these steps to use the DynamoDB console to query data in the Music table.


Open the DynamoDB console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/dynamodb/.


In the navigation pane on the left side of the console, choose Tables.


Choose the Music table from the table list.


Choose the Items tab for the Music table.


In the drop-down list, choose Query.



For Partition key, enter Acme Band, and then choose Start search.
