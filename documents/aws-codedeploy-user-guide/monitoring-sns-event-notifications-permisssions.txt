Grant Amazon SNS permissions to a CodeDeploy service role
Before your triggers can generate notifications, the service role you use in your CodeDeploy operations must be granted permission to access the Amazon SNS resources. 
To grant Amazon SNS permissions to a service role


Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the IAM console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/iam/.


In the IAM console, in the navigation pane, choose Roles.


Choose the name of the service role you use in your AWS CodeDeploy operations.


On the Permissions tab, in the Inline Policies area, choose Create Role Policy.


–or–
If the Create Role Policy button is not available, expand the Inline Policies area, and then choose click here.


On the Set Permissions page, choose Custom Policy, and then choose Select.


On the Review Policy page, in the Policy Name field, enter a name to identify this policy, such as SNSPublish.


Paste the following into the Policy Document field: 


{
       "Version": "2012-10-17",
       "Statement": [
           {
               "Effect": "Allow",
               "Action": "sns:Publish",
               "Resource": "*"
           }
       ]
   }

Choose Apply Policy.
