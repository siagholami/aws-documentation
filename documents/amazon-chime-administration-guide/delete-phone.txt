Deleting phone numbers
Delete unassigned phone numbers from your phone number management Inventory. For more information about unassigning phone numbers, see Managing phone number inventory.
To delete unassigned phone numbers


Open the Amazon Chime console at https://chime.aws.amazon.com/.


For Calling, choose Phone number management.


Choose Inventory, and select the phone number or numbers to delete.


For Actions, choose Delete phone number(s).


Select the check box, and choose Delete.


Deleted phone numbers are held in the Deletion queue for 7 days before they are deleted permanently.
