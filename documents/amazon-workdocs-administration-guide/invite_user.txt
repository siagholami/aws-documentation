To invite new users


In the admin control panel, under Manage Users, choose Invite Users.


In the Invite Users dialog box, for Who would you like to invite?, type the invitee's email address, and choose Send. Repeat this step for each invitation.


An invitation email is sent to each recipient with a link and instructions about how to create an Amazon WorkDocs account. The invitation link expires after 30 days.
