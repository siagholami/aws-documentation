Sending messages
You can send an email message to the people with whom you have already shared a file.
To send an email message


Open the file in Amazon WorkDocs.


Choose Share, Permissions, Email people you've shared with. 


Type your message and choose OK.
Note
By default, your email is sent from “Amazon WorkDocs no45reply@amazon46awsapps46com,” an unmonitored email address. Contributor email addresses are not included.
