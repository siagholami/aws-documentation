How to Enable MFA
Use the following procedure to enable MFA in the AWS SSO console. Before you enable MFA, we recommend that you first review details about Authentication Methods.
To enable MFA


Open the AWS SSO console.


In the left navigation pane, choose Settings.


On the Settings page, choose Configure.


On the Configure authentication page, choose one of the following Authentication Methods based on your business needs:
    Only when their sign-in context changes (context-aware)
    Every time they sign in (always-on)


Choose Save Changes.
