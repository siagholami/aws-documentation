Track events as customers interact with contact flows
Amazon Connect contact flow logs provide you with real-time details about events in your contact flows as customers interact with them. You can also use contact flow logs to help debug your contact flows as you are creating them. If needed, you can always roll back to a previous version of a contact flow.
