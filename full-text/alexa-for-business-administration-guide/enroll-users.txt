Set up Enrollment
Before you can invite users, you must first set up user enrollment. 
To set up user enrollment


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose Settings, User enrollment, Edit.


For Company Name, enter the name of your company.


For Company contact email address, enter the full email address that your invited users can contact if they have any questions while going through the enrollment process.


Choose Save.


You can edit the company name, company contact email, featured private skills, and featured public skills at any time.
Note
Any invitations that have been sent before you make edits displays old information in both the email and the online webpage that a user navigates to during enrollment. 
To edit the user enrollment email


Open the Alexa for Business console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/a4b/.


Choose User Enrollment, Edit.


Edit the values for Company name, Company contact email address, Featured private skills, or Featured public skills.


Choose Save.
