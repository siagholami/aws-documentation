Modifying the Transform
When you move the manipulator to another location in the viewport, you can modify the scale, rotation, or translation of the entity. This feature makes it easier for you to manage your entities. 
To modify the transform


In the viewport, select an entity. The manipulator appears.


Click and drag the manipulator to transform the selected entity.


Press and hold Ctrl, and click and drag the manipulator to another location.


Use the manipulator again to adjust the scale, rotation, or translation of the entity.
