Adding a Terrain Texture Layer
Create a new texture layer and change the terrain texture and material as desired.
To add a terrain texture layer


In Lumberyard Editor, click Tools, Other, Terrain Texture Layers.


In Terrain Texture Layers, under Layer Tasks, click Add Layer.


Double-click the NewLayer text and assign a unique name to it.


Click Materials/material_terrain_default to open Material Editor.


In the tree, click Materials\material_terrain_default and adjust material and other settings as needed.
