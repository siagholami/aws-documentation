Video Playback Gem
With the Video Playback gem, you can play videos in a game or level loading screen, or on textures in your Lumberyard level. When you enable the Video Playback gem, the Video Playback component is enabled. For instructions on how to prepare Lumberyard for video playback, and how to set up video playback in your level, see Video Playback.
For more information on playing videos in load screens, see Defining Game and Level Load Screens.
