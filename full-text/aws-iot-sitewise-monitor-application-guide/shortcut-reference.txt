Keyboard and mouse shortcuts
You can use the following shortcuts to quickly perform certain tasks in AWS IoT SiteWise Monitor.
Shortcut reference 



Shortcut
Description




Double-click mouse
Zoom in a visualization at the selected point.


Press Shift and double-click mouse
Zoom out a visualization at the selected point.


Drag mouse
Zoom in a visualization to a selected time range.


Press Shift and drag mouse
Shift a visualization's time range left or right.
