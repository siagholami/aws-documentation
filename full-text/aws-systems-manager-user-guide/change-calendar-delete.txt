Delete a Change Calendar entry
You can delete a Change Calendar entry by using either the AWS Management Console or the AWS CLI. Deleting a Change Calendar entry deletes all associated events.
Delete a Change Calendar entry (console)


Open the AWS Systems Manager console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/systems-manager/.


In the navigation pane, choose Change Calendar.


In the Change Calendar list, choose the name of the calendar entry that you want to delete.


On the calendar entry's details page, choose Delete. When you are prompted to confirm that you want to delete the calendar entry, choose Delete.
