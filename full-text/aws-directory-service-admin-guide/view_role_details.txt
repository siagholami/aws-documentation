Viewing Users and Groups Assigned to a Role
To view the users and groups assigned to a role, perform the following steps.
To view users and group assigned to a role


In the AWS Directory Service console navigation pane, choose Directories.


On the Directories page, choose your directory ID.


On the Directory details page, select the Application management tab.


Under the AWS Management Console section, choose the role you want to view. 


On the Selected role page, under Manage users and groups for this role, you can view the users and groups assigned to the role.
