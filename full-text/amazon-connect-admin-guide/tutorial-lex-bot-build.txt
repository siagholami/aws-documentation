Part 4: Build and test the Amazon Lex bot
Build and test your bot to make sure that it works as intended before you publish it.


In the Amazon Lex console, choose Build. The build may take a minute or two.



When it's finished building, choose Test Chatbot.



Test the PasswordReset intent. In the Test Chatbot pane, type I forgot my password, and press Enter.
