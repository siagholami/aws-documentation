How do I change the language of the AWS Management Console?
You can change the display language of the AWS Management Console. Several languages are supported.
To change the console language


Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open the Amazon S3 console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/s3/.


Scroll down until you see the bar at the bottom of the window, and then choose the language on the left side of the bar. 



Choose the language that you want from the menu. This will change the langauge for the entire AWS Management Console.
