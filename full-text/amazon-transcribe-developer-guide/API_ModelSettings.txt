ModelSettings
The object used to call your custom language model to your transcription job.
Contents
LanguageModelName 
The name of your custom language model.
Type: String
Length Constraints: Minimum length of 1. Maximum length of 200.
Pattern: ^[0-9a-zA-Z._-] 
Required: No
