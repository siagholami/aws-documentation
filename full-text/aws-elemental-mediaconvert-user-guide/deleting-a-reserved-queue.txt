Deleting a reserved queue in AWS Elemental MediaConvert
You can delete any queue other than the default queue.
Note
You can't delete a reserved queue that has an active pricing plan or that contains unprocessed jobs.
To delete a reserved queue


Open the MediaConvert console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/mediaconvert.


On the navigation bar of the MediaConvert console, choose the Region where you created the queue that you want to delete.



Choose the three-bar icon on the left to access the left navigation pane.


Choose Queues.


On the Queues page, in the Reserved queues section, choose the option next to the name of the queue that you want to delete.


Choose Actions, Delete reserved queue.
