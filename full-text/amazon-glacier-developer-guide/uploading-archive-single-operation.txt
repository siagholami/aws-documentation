Uploading an Archive in a Single Operation
As described in Uploading an Archive in Amazon S3 Glacier, you can upload smaller archives in a single operation. However, we encourage Amazon S3 Glacier (S3 Glacier) customers to use Multipart Upload to upload archives greater than 100 MB.
