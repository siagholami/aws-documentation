Rename a Graph on a CloudWatch Dashboard
You can change the default name that CloudWatch assigns to a graph on your dashboard.
To rename a graph on a dashboard


Open the CloudWatch console at https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudwatch/.


In the navigation pane, choose Dashboards and select a dashboard.


Hover over the title of the graph and choose Widget actions and Edit.


On the Edit graph screen, near the top, choose the title of the graph.


For Title, enter a new name and choose Ok (check mark). In the lower-right corner of the Edit graph screen, choose Update widget.
